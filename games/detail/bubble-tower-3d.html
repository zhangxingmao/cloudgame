About This Game

Bubble Tower 3D is an exciting bubble shooter puzzle game that takes the classic gameplay to a whole new dimension. Aim, shoot, and match colorful bubbles to clear the tower and progress through challenging levels.

Unlike traditional bubble shooters, Bubble Tower 3D features a three-dimensional tower that you can rotate to find the best angle for your shots. The stunning 3D graphics and smooth animations create an immersive puzzle experience that's both relaxing and challenging.

With numerous levels of increasing difficulty, special power-ups, and strategic gameplay elements, Bubble Tower 3D offers endless hours of bubble-popping fun. Match three or more bubbles of the same color to make them pop, and watch as gravity pulls down any bubbles that are no longer supported!

How to Play

Bubble Tower 3D features simple, intuitive controls that make it easy to play:

  • Aim: Drag to aim your bubble shooter at the tower
  • Shoot: Release to fire a bubble toward the tower
  • Rotate: Swipe left or right to rotate the tower and find the best angle
  • Match: Connect three or more bubbles of the same color to pop them

Tips for Success:

  • Always aim for clusters of the same color to maximize your points
  • Use power-ups strategically to clear difficult sections
  • Remember to rotate the tower to find hidden clusters of matching bubbles
  • Try to remove supporting bubbles to trigger chain reactions
  • Plan your shots carefully - the more bubbles you clear in one shot, the higher your score
